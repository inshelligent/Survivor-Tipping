{{ title }} - who's going home tonight?

Rules

The competition allows you to vote on who will go home at tribal councils for the current season of Survivor. Only one vote per tribal is allowed.

Scoring

Choose the person to that is eliminated at the selected tribal council as your first vote and you will receive 10 points, as your second vote 3 points, or as your third vote 1 point.

Steps for placing a vote

  • Select the date of the Tribal council. Only future tribals will be available for selection.
  • Select the person you believe is most likely to be eliminated as your first vote. First vote is a required field!
  • Optionally, you may choose to select a second and third vote. This can be the same contestant as your first vote, or other contestants.
